About

Egerador is a beta game generation application, and is committed to generate numbers for you to play in the mega-sena(Lottery), but it does not guarantee success, so there is chance of the generated game not being drawn in the mega-sena(Lottery), the aim is to help build a game that can, perhaps, getting 4 to 6 numbers right, but the chances of not getting it right are great, so keep in mind that the generated game may not win the mega-sena(Lottery).

This is a free version and allows you to generate all 6 numbers automatically, or generate just a few numbers by clicking on the generate game button, the rest being From the numbers provided by the player, you can also enter all 6 numbers, check the option 'not to repeat the game' and click on Generate Game to see if your game has already been drawn. The option 'not to repeat game' guarantees that the generated game was not drawn in the mega-scene. It sends out a game that has already been drawn if you enter the 6 numbers. There is a button to clear the numbers, and a button to annotate the mounted games, and in each annotated game it is possible to delete the game. Annotated games can be accessed in Notes below next to the contact link or in the menu above

Annotated games can be deleted, but are not saved for future reference in this free version. The numbers are not shared or seen by other users of the site
